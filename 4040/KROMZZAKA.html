KROMZZAKA

Structure

The organisation of USRM is pretty straight forward. A central council, with the help of a administrative helping organisation is in charge of managing the country as a whole, that is forging diplomatic relations, planning the economy and making laws. Smaller administrative agents are then responsable for managing the individual settlements, in which the state is divided.

History

The Socio de Liberaj Nacioj strongly funded construction and encouraged settlements on the red planet and other locations in the inner solar system since the early 2000 as a response to a predicted raw material and housing shortage on Earth in the far future. Even though this program, "Osborne Program" was widely criticized for it's astronomical cost and it's hazy goal, it lead to a lot of Companies investing into transportation and infrastructure on mars, making it near self-sufficient and even comfortable to live on. During the first half of the 20th century then, Mars was the hotspot of various technological field with multiple important companies, like General Astronautics and Konstruo Anton kaj Rouge.

Demography and Population

The USRM is economically and physically made out of several settlements on the surface of Mars, as well as in orbit around the planet and one on either of it's smaller Moons. Every settlement has it's unique function, be it agriculture, industry, transport, military or even administration/culture.

Territories

The USRM controls the entire surface of Mars, Phobos Deimos and the massive and flexible cosmic fleet has a big control over large ammounts of space, especially in the astroid belt region.

Military

The military is divided into martian ground assault forces and the red fleet. Both are under direct command from the central council. It is worth an article on it's own

Foreign Relations

Culturally and informationwise, the USRM rather likes to be more isolated, but it likes trading stuff it can do well or doesn't want with stuff it needs. It refuses however direct transactions, because it distrusts tradittional foreign currency, whitch can make trade very tedious.

Agriculture & Industry

The USRM concentrates its efforts maily on industry, transportation industry after all. Nearly all space craft, except for terrestrian and venus military ones are build by the USRM. The same is true for most of the ground vehicles, atmospheric transport vehiecles and even some classical "liquid" ships. Space ships and products to be exported get build exclusively in orbit, becaus surface-orbit transportation is the limiting factor on interplanetary transport. The agriculture is based on hydroponics and does not focus only on food, but also on oxygen production. The whole agricultural system of Mars is a complex self-soustaining biochemical and agricultural system which uses water from the poles, methane and CO2 from the Atmosphere to make oxygen and every kind of food for a healthy diet. The plant used for oxygen production is a special kind of gene.manipulated canabis, which is very small and can runn on few nutriments.

Trade & Transport

For Surface to space transportation, mars uses, similar to Earth, launch loops and occasional spaceplane transport. Mars also has established a proper space lift who has a greater transport density. Between the settlements on the surface extend both a highspeed and a bulk train network, on which goods and people can be transported. The big tarins are exclusively powered directly by nuclear power.

Education

All citicens get to enjoy the basic education, which consist of 5 grades in which reading, writing and mathematics is thaught, with a strong focus on math. Then, the children go through 7 years of testing, in which they are supposed to get "filtered". The classes are made specifically so that a third of the students fail at least 1 year and have to resort to a lower education. At their 3 year of these 7, the students get to choose a field. The options are:

Engeneering: The backbone of the economy: this reaches from simple mechanics and industry workers up to researchers and chief engeniers
Applied Science: The people who are respnsable for life support and medical threatment
Military: Soldiers and Officers
Administration: By far the smallest group. These include logistic, local adminitrative and political professions

The ones left by the end get then the option to proceed with a higher education at their profession, where they can make their career.

Infrastructure

The planet's infrastructures consists entirly of the settlements and the infrastructures that connects them. The settlements are over all connected through a train system, which started construction since the very beginning of mars colonisations in the 1980s. Oxygen, water and and other fluids get transported through a extensive pipeline system spanning the whole planet. Because the pipeline transport vital products, there is almost never one single connection. As soon as a pipeline is dammaged, there are multiple alternative backup pipelines over which water and oxygen can be rerouted. The most important pipelines are even defended by armored stations and and mobile squadrons, ready to strike at every time in case of wartime or terrorist actions. Furthermore ground-space connection is ensured by launch loops and a space elevator
