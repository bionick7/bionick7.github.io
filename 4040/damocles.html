Damocles Sword

With growing wealth and autonomy, the cis-jovian state found itself with outdated and disjointed naval defenses and little to no offensive capability. This eventually lead to the development of the 'Damocles Sword' and her sister-ship, the 'Inevitable arrival'. The Damocles Sword class was based on the martian navies tactical artillery and expanded to fit the role of a dedicated flagship. The general idea was to have a mobile high-Dv, high firepower flagship to bolster older defenses wherever a battle was expected. The development of the ship was long and troubled as a lot of habitation and power management systems had to be reworked, in addition to adding the state -of-the-art electronic warfare and sensor capabilities and the insistence of the cis-jovian insistence to produce everything locally. In addition to it's traditional role, the quick response time, together with it's powerful reactor, diverse accommodations, large lazaret and overdesigned agricultural arrays lends this ship well to humanitarian operations.

High-tech pride

The Communication & intel Acquisition & Processing device (CIAP) is the pride of Lagrange labs and a milestone for jovian technological achievements. The device can monitor most major traffic in the jovian system passively while hidden and in radio silence and respond to an oncoming attack after the attacker already committed to the trajectory.When on the offensive, the dish can be used to communicate with almost anything anywhere in the jovian (political) sphere of influence

Designed for kinetic penetration

The defining feature of the tactical artillery that the ship is based on was the large, GJ spinal rail gun the rest of the ship is build around. In combat, the reactor is able to peak at 2 GW to fire the spinal gun 2x per second, making full use of the massive high-power sails that define the silhouette
